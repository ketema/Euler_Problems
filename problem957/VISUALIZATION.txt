================================================================================
HOW TWO SEGMENTS CROSS (Interleaving Orders)
================================================================================

Example: Do (r1, b1) and (r2, b2) cross?

Step 1: Check order around r1
------------------------------------------------------------

         Cyclic order around r1: [b1, b2]

                    r1
                    ●
                   /|\
                  / | \
            [1]→ /  |  \ ←[2]
               b1   |   b2
                ●   |    ●

         At r1: b1 comes BEFORE b2


Step 2: Check order around r2
------------------------------------------------------------

         Cyclic order around r2: [b2, b1]

                    r2
                    ●
                   /|\
                  / | \
            [1]→ /  |  \ ←[2]
               b2   |   b1
                ●   |    ●

         At r2: b2 comes BEFORE b1 (REVERSED!)


Step 3: Interleaving Test
------------------------------------------------------------

  Order at r1: b1 < b2  (b1 first)
  Order at r2: b2 < b1  (b2 first)

  Orders DISAGREE → Segments INTERLEAVE → CROSSING!


Visualization of Crossing:
------------------------------------------------------------

        ●                     ●

        | \                 / |

    b1  |    \    ●!    /    |  b1
     ●--|-----\   ↑   /-----|--●

  Segment (r1→b1) crosses segment (r2→b2)
  Segment (r1→b2) crosses segment (r2→b1)

  For 1 pair of blues + 1 pair of disagreeing reds:
    → 1 crossing (new blue created)
